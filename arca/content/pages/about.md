# About Arca

Arca is a revolutionary content management system that takes a different approach to organizing information. Instead of complex databases and proprietary formats, we store everything in plain Markdown files with YAML front matter.

## Our Vision

We believe that content should be:

- **Accessible** - Easy to create, edit, and distribute
- **Portable** - Not locked into proprietary systems
- **Versionable** - Trackable with standard version control tools
- **Durable** - Resistant to technology changes and obsolescence

## Our Team

Arca was created by a small team of passionate developers who were tired of complex, bloated content management systems. We wanted something lightweight but powerful, simple but flexible.

## Technology

Arca is built using:

- **Markdown** for all content
- **YAML** for structured metadata
- **Git** for version control
- **HTMX** for dynamic interfaces without complex JavaScript
- **FastAPI** for our lean backend services
